📱 Product Analytics 💳 FinTech · UPI

Mobile App Funnel Collapse Detector

End-to-end product analytics on a UPI bill-payment app — identifying the exact funnel crisis point, validating a UI redesign via A/B test, and defining the North Star Metric that predicts 30-day retention.

Business Problem
Despite being live 6 months, only ~10% of users activate Bill Payment Automation. The team needed to know exactly where users drop off, whether a UI redesign works, and which event best predicts long-term retention.
Dataset & Scope
50,000 users over 90 days · 200K+ event rows · 8 funnel steps · 50/50 A/B split · 5 acquisition channels · 3 city tiers across Android & iOS.
Key Findings
  • →Step 2 (Address Verification) converts only 42.9% — single largest drop-off, consistent across all devices and city tiers. Core design problem, not network issue.
  • →New UI A/B test: +27.7% relative lift (11.37% vs 8.90%), p < 0.000001, SRM passed. Recommendation: Ship to 100% of users immediately.
  • →North Star Metric: automation_activated within 7 days — Spearman r = 0.607 with Day 30 retention. No other event comes close.
  • →Retention drops 55% from Day 1 (44%) to Day 7 (19%) — product not creating habit-forming moments in first week.

🏥 Healthcare Operations 🤖 Forecasting · ML

Hospital Patient Demand Forecasting & Staffing Intelligence

End-to-end forecasting system for a 500-bed Chennai hospital — predicting daily OPD volumes 14–30 days ahead using Facebook Prophet with real Indian holiday API, converting forecasts into shift-wise staffing plans via Erlang C, and quantifying ₹3–4L/month cost leakage from flat scheduling.

Business Problem
A multi-specialty hospital uses fixed monthly staffing schedules — blind to demand patterns. Mondays are 35% busier than Fridays. Monsoon months spike 20–25%. Result: chronic SLA breaches and ₹3–4 Lakhs wasted monthly on misaligned staffing.
Solution Approach
Facebook Prophet + Calendarific REST API for real Indian holiday regressors → Erlang C staffing formula with 30% shrinkage → cost leakage model → deployed as a live Streamlit web app accessible to non-technical hospital managers.
Key Findings
  • →Prophet achieved ~9% MAPE — 47% better than naive same-day-last-week baseline (17%). Trained on 1,066 days with 60-day temporal holdout.
  • →Monday mornings need 40% more staff than Friday evenings — flat scheduling creates predictable SLA breaches every week at 9–11AM.
  • →Day before major Indian holidays spikes 15–20%; the holiday itself drops 30–40%. Flat scheduling is overstaffed on the holiday and understaffed the day before — simultaneously.
  • →Forecast-based scheduling projected to save ₹36–48 Lakhs annually through elimination of overtime and SLA breach penalties.

💳 FinTech · Credit Risk 🤖 AI-Powered

MSME Loan Default Risk Intelligence System

Simulating an NBFC credit risk analyst — flagging high-risk borrowers 60–90 days before default using XGBoost, SHAP, and AI-generated risk narratives.

Business Problem
An Indian NBFC with a 5,000-borrower MSME portfolio faces a rising NPA rate approaching 8% — triggering RBI compliance risk. The credit team needs a data-driven early warning system to flag high-risk borrowers 60–90 days before default for proactive intervention.
Dataset & Scope
Custom-generated 5,000-row MSME loan dataset with 21 features — Indian states, sectors, GST filing patterns, loan amounts in INR, DPD buckets, EMI bounce counts, collateral types. Calibrated to realistic NBFC portfolio: 20% default rate, 5.12% NPA rate.
Approach
Data generation & cleaning → Feature engineering (EMI stress flag, DPD buckets, credit bands) → SQL credit risk queries (PAR 30/60/90, branch scorecards, GST default correlation) → XGBoost PD model with SHAP → 2-page Power BI dashboard → AI risk narrative generator.
AI Integration
Built an LLM-powered risk narrative generator that converts raw PD scores into plain-English borrower summaries for relationship managers — e.g. "Immediate escalation required: 6 EMI bounces, 97% default probability, ₹50L exposure backed by Inventory collateral."
Key Insights Discovered
  • →Retail Trade GST Non-Filers default at 27.94% — nearly 40% above the 20% portfolio average — making GST filing status a free, high-signal input for loan origination scoring.
  • →Borrowers with 3+ EMI bounces in 6 months show 45%+ default rates — the single strongest behavioral early-warning signal, outperforming credit score at equivalent threshold.
  • →BR_05 and BR_02 branches exceed 23% branch default rates — requiring immediate collection task force deployment and loan officer performance review.
  • →Agri Processing sector holds the highest default rate at 23.36%, driven by seasonal revenue volatility and monsoon dependency — recommending tighter underwriting and reduced max ticket size.

🛒 E-Commerce · Retail 🤖 AI-Powered

E-Commerce Performance Analytics — Olist Brazil

Analyzing 96K orders across 2 years of Brazilian e-commerce data — uncovering revenue drivers, delivery performance gaps, and customer review patterns.

Business Problem
Olist, Brazil's largest e-commerce marketplace, needed clarity on which product categories drive revenue, why late deliveries spike in certain months, and how review scores track against operational performance — to prioritize seller quality improvements.
Dataset & Scope
96,000+ order records from the Olist Brazil public dataset spanning Sep 2016 – Aug 2018. KPIs tracked: ₹15.42M total revenue, 13.81 avg delivery days, 4.07 avg review score, 91.89% on-time delivery rate across 7,826 late orders.
Approach
EDA on monthly revenue trends, product category breakdown, city-level seller revenue, on-time delivery analysis, and review score tracking. Built interactive Power BI dashboard with order month slicer, donut chart for delivery splits, and multi-page comparative analysis.
Key Visuals Built
Monthly Revenue Trend (line), Top Categories by Revenue (bar), On-Time vs Late Deliveries (donut), Avg Review Score by Month (bar), On-Time Delivery % by Month, Top Cities by Seller Revenue, Late Orders by Month, Orders by Day/Hour, Payment Method split.
Key Insights Discovered
  • →Health & Beauty is the top revenue-generating category at ~₹1M, followed by Watches/Gifts and Bed/Bath — indicating strong lifestyle product demand driving platform growth.
  • →On-time delivery rate stabilized at ~95%+ from late 2017 onward after an early spike of 50+ avg delivery days in Oct 2016 — showing clear operational improvement.
  • →São Paulo sellers dominate revenue at over ₹1M — 3x the next city — creating geographic seller concentration risk and suggesting expansion opportunity in underserved states.
  • →Monday and Tuesday show peak order volumes (~19K each), while Sunday is lowest (~14K) — enabling targeted marketing spend scheduling for maximum ROAS.
  • →73.77% of orders paid via credit card with peak ordering hours between 10AM–3PM — critical inputs for payment infrastructure planning and flash sale scheduling.
